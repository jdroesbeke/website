Privacy Policy

At ACSI Services, we attach great importance to the protection of your personal data. In this policy, we explain which data we process and why.

§ 1 Which data do we process?

When you use our services or contact us, we may process personal data such as your name, telephone number, email address, and information you provide yourself. We use this data solely to carry out our services, enable communication, and improve our service.

§ 2 Processing of conversations via AI

As part of our services, telephone conversations are processed using automated systems and AI technology. This enables us to handle conversations correctly, qualify leads, and manage appointments. This processing takes place within the framework of our agreement and to optimize our services.

§ 3 Sharing with third parties

We only share personal data with third parties when this is necessary for providing our services, such as when using software, hosting, or telephony integrations. These parties process data solely on behalf of ACSI Services and comply with applicable privacy legislation.

§ 4 Retention periods

We do not retain personal data longer than necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, unless a legal retention obligation requires otherwise.

§ 5 Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Have your personal data corrected
  • Have your personal data deleted
  • Object to the processing of your data

Requests can be submitted via ops@acsi-services.com.

§ 6 Security

ACSI Services takes appropriate measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

§ 7 Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to amend this privacy policy. The most current version is always available on our website.

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us via ops@acsi-services.com. See also our terms and conditions.
