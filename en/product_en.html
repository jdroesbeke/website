How it works

From first call to appointment

This is how every conversation unfolds, from the first contact to the confirmation in your calendar. Professional, consistent, and fully automated.

1

Immediate answering

When a customer calls, the call is immediately picked up by the digital assistant. No waiting time, no missed call. The caller is professionally greeted in a way that matches your business, making the conversation feel familiar from the very first moment.

2

Determining the need

The assistant understands the caller’s question or need. Instead of only giving an answer and ending the conversation, the assistant purposefully guides the conversation forward. Relevant questions are asked to determine the next logical step.

3

Qualifying the lead

When the caller is interested, they are automatically qualified. The assistant gathers the right information in a natural way within the conversation, without it feeling like a form or interrogation. Type of request, preferences, relevant details: everything is recorded.

4

Scheduling the appointment

The assistant immediately checks the calendar for available times and offers them. As soon as a time is chosen, the appointment is directly recorded and confirmed in your Google Calendar or Outlook, fully automatically, without manual work.

5

Confirmation & follow-up

After the conversation, the customer receives a confirmation message. With the Premium package, reminders are sent to prevent no-shows. The information from the conversation is stored in a structured way for further follow-up and reporting.

6

No loose ends

Leads that have not yet directly resulted in an appointment are not dismissed. Based on the information from the conversation, follow-up can be structured. This creates a continuous flow, with no missed opportunities.
